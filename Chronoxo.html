What we do

Cronoxo is derived from the word 'Cronos' in greek which is 'time' in english. Our brand revolves around catering watches for the elderly

VISION: Help seniors get by everyday with ease while using our watches

We aim to create watches for the elderly using the latest technology available while keeping the design old fashined to cater the elderly

What are we trying to solve?

  • The struggle to read without glasses
  • Loneliness
  • To measure and keep track of the users medical problems
    •

    Watch Functions

  • AI voice can imitate their childrens or family's voices using a Cronoxos voices replication algorithm.
  • Track users' locations and transmit them to family's phones simultaneously
  • Track users' heart rate, temperature, coughs and sleep cycles. This data will also me transmitted to their family and assigned doctor.
  • Automativally call an ambulance and close family members when a fall has been detected or when it notices heart rate to be abnormally high or low.
  • Calculate regular meal values and provides dietary advices to guide the user's daily life.
  • Watch AI will read out the words in a book by just touching them with your finger hence reducing the time and effort wasted keeping glasses on the user.
  • Control all the smart houshold appliances by saying the command
